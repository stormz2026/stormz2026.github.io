About The Game

A lone special forces operative finds himself trapped in an evacuated war-torn urban zone. A secret military experiment has gone catastrophically wrong, releasing a bio-agent that doesn't kill—it mutates.

Former comrades and enemy soldiers are now zombified warriors. Though dead, they've retained their armor and combat instincts, making them deadlier than any ordinary undead. The city is desolate, streets filled with rubble, destroyed vehicles, and raging fires. There's no backup. The only objective is to survive the onslaught and eliminate the threat.

Gameplay

Top-Down Combat

Experience intense action from a tactical bird's-eye view. Independent movement and aiming allow you to shoot while retreating, creating dynamic combat scenarios.

Wave System

Fight through increasingly challenging waves of zombified soldiers. Each wave brings more enemies and greater intensity. Brief respites between waves let you reposition and prepare.

Ammo Management

Ammunition is finite and precious. Every shot counts. Scavenge ammo from fallen enemies and supply crates scattered throughout the war-torn streets.

Key Features

🎯

Tactical Top-Down Action

Master the art of positioning and movement in intense isometric combat. Keyboard movement with mouse aiming creates fluid, skill-based gameplay.

⚡

Fast-Paced Combat

Quick reflexes and strategic thinking are your only weapons against the relentless zombie horde. Simple yet deep mechanics create addictive gameplay.

🏆

Score-Based Progression

Every eliminated enemy adds to your score. Compete against yourself and push for higher waves. How long can you survive?

🧟

Zombified Soldiers

Face different enemy types: standard soldiers that come in groups and fast runners who've retained their physical prowess. Each requires different tactics.
